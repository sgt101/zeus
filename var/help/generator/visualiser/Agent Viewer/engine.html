Coordination Engine Viewer On-Line Help

What is it?
The Coordination Engine Viewer is a component that displays the goals that are being pursued by this agent. It is displays the state of the goals in terms of:
  • Ready - the goal is ready to be processed.
  • Not ready - the goal is not yet ready to be processed.
  • Running - the goal is being processed.
  • Failed - the processing of the goal has failed.
  • Waiting - processing of the goal has been suspended.
  • Goal - the goal has been sucessfully processed.

How do I use it?
The Coordination Engine Viewer consists of two panes. When you select an entry in the top pane (i.e. a goal), its composition and their respective states are displayed in the bottom pane.
