Planner & Scheduler Viewer On-Line Help

What is it?
The Planner & Scheduler Viewer is a component that displays the agent's diary of scheduled goals. The diary is a two-dimesional array, with time on one dimension and processors on another. The agent schedules its activities over a finitie time period defined by the length of its diary, and across a finite number of processors, given the width of the diary.

How do I use it?
The Planner & Scheduler Viewer consists of two panes. The diary is in the top pane and the bottom pane provides a diagnostic information of an entry in the diary. Thus when you click on an entry in the diary, its corresponding diagnostic information will be displayed in the bottom pane. The top pane displays the ten most current entries in the diary. You can change this setting by specifying a range (less than default value of ten). The range is specified when you key in a value in the to textfield. The value must be greater or equal to the current time. To view the new settings click on the Set button. Clicking on the Reset will change the setting to the default value of ten.
